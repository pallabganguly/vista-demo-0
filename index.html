IIM Bangalore's VISTA

India's largest B-school summit

Vista, IIM Bangalore’s Annual Business Summit, has been a celebration of ideas and leaders, that shape the face of business and the global economy, for the past two decades. Join us for a new chapter in the VISTA story!

Speaker Sessions

Speakers for VISTA 2023 include: Peyush Bansal, CEO, Flipkart, Aman Gupta, CMO, boAt, Aamir Khan, Boman Irani, and more!

Aamir Khan

In a career spanning over 30 years, countless acclaimed awards, and movies influencing generations, Mr. Aamir Khan has established himself as one of the most insightful and influential people in the Indian cinema industry. Register today for Mr. Khan's talk at VISTA here.

Boman Irani

Mr Boman Irani is the recipient of numerous awards from institutions like Filmfare, Zee Cinema, Star Screen, Stardust and IIFA. He is known for playing both comedic and villainous roles in Bollywood films. Register today for Mr. Irani's talk at VISTA here.

Aman Gupta

Mr Aman Gupta is the Co-Founder and CMO at boAt Lifestyle, a consumer electronics brand focusing on audio technology headwear. He is known for his appearance on the hit TV show "Shark Tank" in which he invests in Indian startups. Register today for Mr. Gupta's talk at VISTA here.

Peyush Bansal

Mr Peyush Bansal is the CEO of Lenskart, one of the leading Indian eyewear retail chains in India. An alumnus of the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, Mr Peyush has transformed the way how people see and experience the world. Register today for Mr. Bansal's talk at VISTA here.

Young Leader's Summit

Young Leaders’ Summit brings together the corporate leaders and young minds who aspire to become future business leaders.

Speaker Series

Participants will get the opportunity to interact with renowned speakers from a host of domains including business, politics, media, and social welfare.

Faculty Workshop

Workshops on communication, business, corporate ethics and much more, held and run by IIM Bangalore's famed faculty!

Case Study Workshop and Competition

Learning through case studies is a pedagogical method widely adopted in business schools worldwide. Learn through hands-on experience of the case-based approach at the YLS workshop.

Domain-Specific Fireside Chat

To get a holistic view of MBA life & beyond, domain-specific fireside chats by current MBA students will be held throughout the summit. These are aimed at giving Young Leaders a glimpse into the world of studying finance, marketing through an informative yet fun & informal lens!
